EXCEL PROJECT

This project focuses on analyzing a pizza shop's 2015 sales performance using Microsoft Excel. The goal was to understand customer purchasing behavior, identify top-performing products, evaluate daily and hourly sales trends, and generate insights that can support business decisions such as inventory planning, staffing, and marketing.

POWER BI PROJECT

This project analyzes a dataset containing medical records of 299 patients diagnosed with heart failure. The goal was to explore clinical factors associated with patient survival, identify risk patterns across different age groups, and visualize how key biomarkers and health conditions contribute to patient outcomes. The dataset includes demographic features, clinical measurements, lifestyle risk factors, and a binary target variable indicating whether the patient died during the follow-up period.
